What donations can clearly support

  • Program supplies: robotics kits, electrical components, 3D printing materials, notebooks, and safety gear.
  • Access and inclusion: reduced-cost participation, transportation support, and materials for students who need them.
  • Measurable outcomes: workshop attendance, projects completed, skills practiced, and stories donors can understand.
  • Responsible stewardship: campaign goals, itemized needs, donation pathways, and follow-up reporting.
  • Partnership readiness: sponsor recognition, volunteer opportunities, in-kind giving, and community collaboration.
